India (Hindi: Bhārat), officially the Republic of India (Hindi: Bhārat Gaṇarājya) is a country in South Asia. Chicken Rice

Rice-based dish which often also includes ingredients like Chicken and veg. Long-grained rice varieties like basmati are often used to prepare pulaos.
Sometimes the rice and other ingredients are cooked together in spices and broth, while at other times the parboiled rice is mixed with the spices, vegetable or meat mixture and stir-fried together.
You can use any cut of chicken for my this recipe but boneless, skinless thighs or breasts are the best. The meat is marinated for only 15 minutes while you prepare the other ingredients.

  • Time: 15 Min
  • Serves: Availabel
  • Price: 100 dkk
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 100 grms
Chicken 100 grms
Oil 10 Ml
Masala Mix 10 grms

Step 1: In a large, nonmetallic bowl, whisk together 1 tablespoon each garlic paste and ginger paste, red chili powder, 3/4 teaspoon salt and lemon juice.
Step 2: Place chicken in the marinade, turning to coat the chicken well. Cling wrap or cover the bowl and keep in the refrigerator for the chicken to marinate for 15 to 20 minutes while you prepare the remainder of the ingredients.
Step 3: Add the chicken stock and stir well. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover and simmer till the rice and chicken are done and the water has almost fully dried up.
